about

I am a creative, artistic and ambitious Graphic Designer with a talent for thinking outside the box and coming up with innovative ideas and designs. Having worked in the design and print industry for over 5 years in Poland, I moved to the UK where I started with ad-hoc freelance work. I then got involved with an e-learning company, taking part in a short-term project aimed at creating high-quality vector illustrations. Eventually, I found employment within a signage company and have been working for them to this day. Quite recently, I have developed an interest for digital design and started to learn HTML, CSS and JavaScript. You can see the first results of my new skills right here - in the form of my own portfolio website. I am now looking for new opportunities that would enable me to fully maximise my potential and take my career to the next level.
